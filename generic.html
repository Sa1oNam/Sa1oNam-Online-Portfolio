About Me

One's Perception

Hello, my name is Sarah. I'm a very passionate drawing and find myself drawing even when I'm preoccupied with something else. I also really enjoy listening to all types of music. Music really corresponds to my emotions and it moves fluently when I'm drawing or when I am learning a new subject. Some things that I like to do outside would be stargazing. I'm intrigued by the universe and how we are the matter that had formed from it. I think it's very special. Even though we may feel small in comparison to the rest of the universe, we should feel important from what I believe-for we are the essence of the universe.
